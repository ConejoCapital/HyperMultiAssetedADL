## üéØ Executive Summary

We replayed 3,239,706 clearinghouse events (fills, funding, ledger updates) from the 20:04:54 UTC snapshot through the end of the 12-minute cascade (21:27:00 UTC). The canonical dataset captures every ADL with real-time account value, leverage, and equity. Key outcomes:

- **ADL targets profit, not leverage** ‚Äì 94.5% of ADL'd positions were profitable (avg +80.6% PNL).
- **Low leverage is no shield** ‚Äì Median real-time leverage was 0.18x; 84.9% of positions carried <1x leverage.
- **Negative-equity accounts quantified** ‚Äì 302 accounts fell below zero equity (‚àí$23.19M absorbed by insurance/HLP reserves).
- **Per-asset coverage stable** ‚Äì ADL delivered ~35% of liquidation notional on every major ticker (mean 35.4%, median 33.2%).
